THE GENESIS

Thailand's No.1 Female DJ and self-made sonic force, Roxy June has earned her title the hard way — on mainstages across 30+ countries. Known as the Queen of Neck Breakers, she has commanded rooms from Los Angeles to London, Sydney to Seoul, and Abu Dhabi to Hong Kong. A headline fixture at World DJ Music Festival, Global Gathering, S2O, Waterzonic, and Don't Let Daddy Know, her sets are visceral, relentless, and impossible to forget.

Winner of the Pioneer Pro DJ Lady Championship 2010, ranked Top 9 Female DJ by DJane Mag, and named #1 Hottest by EDMdroid — the accolades follow the work, not the other way around. As a V-MODA Brand Ambassador and UDG endorsee, she carries the full weight of the craft. In the studio, her productions speak the same language as her sets: Back N Forth, Hit That, and Let Them Fight — each one a statement, none of them asking permission.
